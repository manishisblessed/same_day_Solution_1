## PART I — INTRODUCTION

### Section 1 — Purpose

**1.1** This Annexure C ("**Fee & Settlement Schedule**") sets out the commission structures, service charges, settlement cycles, cut-off timings, instant settlement fees, minimum payout thresholds, and related financial terms applicable to Channel Partners of SAMEDAY SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED ("**SAMEDAY**").

**1.2** This Schedule forms an integral part of the Channel Partner Agreement and is read together with DOC-1 (Onboarding Form), Annexure A (Compliance Policy), and Annexure B (Role & Service Schedule).

**1.3** All amounts are denominated in Indian Rupees (INR) unless otherwise stated. GST shall apply as per applicable law and is borne by the party specified in each clause.

## PART II — DEFINITIONS

### Section 2 — Definitions

**2.1** "**Business Day**" means a day on which banks in Mumbai and New Delhi are open for business, excluding Saturdays (unless notified as working), Sundays, and public holidays declared by RBI.

**2.2** "**Settlement Cycle**" or "**T+1**" means settlement on the first Business Day following the transaction date (T), subject to cut-off compliance and reconciliation.

**2.3** "**Instant Settlement**" means accelerated credit of eligible transaction proceeds to the Channel Partner's registered bank account on the same day (T+0) or within hours, subject to fees and eligibility criteria in Section 8.

**2.4** "**Net Settlement Amount**" means gross transaction value minus MDR/charges, reversals, chargebacks, holdbacks, penalties, and applicable fees.

**2.5** "**Commission**" means the remuneration payable by SAMEDAY to the Channel Partner for facilitating transactions, expressed as flat amount, percentage, or tiered structure per tables below.

**2.6** "**MDR**" (Merchant Discount Rate) means the fee deducted from transaction value for payment processing, where applicable.

## PART V — HOLIDAYS AND EXCEPTIONS

### Section 12 — Holiday and Non-Business Day Treatment

**12.1** Settlement shall not be processed on RBI-declared public holidays, national holidays, and bank holidays in Mumbai/New Delhi.

**12.2** Transactions on holidays shall be included in the settlement batch of the **next Business Day**, subject to applicable cut-off rules.

**12.3** Holiday Calendar Reference: Channel Partners shall refer to the SAMEDAY partner portal holiday calendar updated annually. Indicative holidays include:

| Holiday | Impact on T+1 Settlement |
|---------|--------------------------|
| Republic Day (26 January) | No settlement; rolls to next Business Day |
| Independence Day (15 August) | No settlement; rolls to next Business Day |
| Gandhi Jayanti (2 October) | No settlement; rolls to next Business Day |
| Diwali / Id / Christmas (as declared) | No settlement; rolls to next Business Day |
| RBI Annual Closing (1 April, if applicable) | No settlement |
| State-specific holidays affecting clearing | As notified on portal |

**12.4** SAMEDAY shall issue advance notification of settlement schedule changes during festival seasons and year-end.

**12.5** Instant Settlement is unavailable on bank holidays. Requests queued on holidays process on the next Business Day.

**12.6** SAMEDAY shall not be liable for delays caused by sponsor bank outages, NPCI downtime, NEFT/IMPS network failures, or force majeure events.

## PART VI — TAXES, INVOICING, AND RECONCILIATION

### Section 13 — GST and TDS

**13.1** Commission paid to Channel Partners is subject to GST as applicable. Channel Partners shall provide valid GSTIN and issue tax invoices/comply with reverse charge mechanism as per law.

**13.2** SAMEDAY may deduct TDS under Section 194H or other applicable provisions at [TDS_RATE]% and issue Form 16A.

**13.3** Service charges and penalties charged by SAMEDAY shall be exclusive of GST; GST shall be added at applicable rate.

### Section 14 — Invoicing

**14.1** Channel Partners shall upload monthly commission invoices on the partner portal by the [INVOICE_DUE_DAY] of the following month for commission paid in the preceding month.

**14.2** Failure to submit invoices may delay commission disbursement for subsequent periods until compliance is restored.

### Section 15 — Reconciliation and Disputes

**15.1** Channel Partners shall reconcile settlement statements within seven (7) Business Days of receipt.

**15.2** Disputes must be raised in writing via [Settlement Dispute Email] with transaction references within fifteen (15) days of settlement date. Late disputes may not be entertained except for proven system errors.

**15.3** SAMEDAY shall investigate disputes within [DISPUTE_RESOLUTION_DAYS] Business Days and credit/debit adjustments in the next settlement cycle if validated.
