## 1. PURPOSE

This Annexure-D records the handover, custody, condition, and return obligations for Point of Sale (POS) and related devices allocated by the Company to the Agent under the Master Service Agreement ("**MSA**"). This Annexure forms an integral part of the MSA and is binding upon execution of the MSA or upon device dispatch/acceptance, whichever is earlier.

---

## 2. OWNERSHIP AND TITLE

2.1 **Title Retention:** All Devices listed in this Annexure remain the sole property of the Company or its financing/leasing partner at all times. No ownership interest passes to the Agent by virtue of handover, usage, deposit, or payment of any fee except where expressly transferred in a separate written instrument signed by authorized Company signatory.

2.2 **Limited License:** The Agent receives a revocable, non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable right to use Devices solely for authorized Services under the MSA.

2.3 **No Encumbrance:** The Agent shall not sell, lease, pledge, mortgage, sub-allocate, or create any third-party interest in Devices.

2.4 **Identification:** The Agent shall not remove, alter, or deface serial numbers, IMEI numbers, asset tags, holograms, or Company branding on Devices.

2.5 **Financing Partner:** Where Devices are subject to third-party financing, the Agent acknowledges that recovery and return obligations extend to such financing partner's rights as notified by the Company.

---

## 3. DEVICE RECOVERY FEE — INR 16,000 + GST

3.1 **Standard Recovery Fee:** In the event of any of the following, the Agent shall pay a **Device Recovery Fee of INR 16,000 (Rupees Sixteen Thousand Only) plus applicable GST per Device**:

| Trigger Event | Recovery Fee Applicable |
|---------------|------------------------|
| Failure to return Device within 7 days of termination/suspension/demand | Yes — INR 16,000 + GST |
| Loss or theft of Device (whether reported or not) | Yes — INR 16,000 + GST |
| Willful damage, tampering, or irreparable damage | Yes — INR 16,000 + GST |
| Unauthorized transfer, sale, or pledge | Yes — INR 16,000 + GST |
| Return of non-functional device due to Agent misuse/negligence | Yes — INR 16,000 + GST |
| Device not recoverable from Downline where Agent allocated device | Yes — INR 16,000 + GST |

3.2 **Recovery Mechanism:** The Company may recover the Device Recovery Fee by debiting Wallet, adjusting settlement/commission, utilizing Reserve, or legal demand without prior consent.

3.3 **Non-Waiver:** Payment of Recovery Fee does not transfer ownership unless expressly agreed in writing.

3.4 **Additional Costs:** Repair costs, logistics, insurance, and legal collection costs may be charged separately where applicable.

3.5 **Multiple Devices:** Recovery Fee applies per Device for each unit not returned or accounted for.

## 6. AGENT OBLIGATIONS DURING CUSTODY

6.1 Use Devices only with Company-approved applications and configurations.

6.2 Maintain Devices in good working order; report faults within twenty-four (24) hours.

6.3 Report loss/theft to Company and police within twenty-four (24) hours; provide FIR copy where available.

6.4 Permit inspection by Company personnel upon reasonable notice or immediately in fraud/compliance cases.

6.5 Not repair, modify, jailbreak, or install unauthorized software.

6.6 Ensure Downline (if any) to whom Device is sub-allocated remains Agent's responsibility; sub-allocation does not transfer custody obligations.

6.7 Maintain adequate insurance for high-risk locations if required by Company notice (optional unless mandated).
