## CLAUSE 8 — POS, EDC CARD ACCEPTANCE, AND OPERATING STANDARDS

8.1 POS and EDC Services include Card, UPI, QR, and related payment acceptance through Company-approved Devices and applications.

8.2 The Partner shall operate Devices only at approved locations reflected in onboarding records and shall notify the Company before relocation. The Partner shall not relocate or remove any Device, signage, or marketing material to other premises without prior written consent.

8.4 **Card processing requirements.** When accepting a Card payment, the Partner shall:
   (a) determine that the Card is valid and not expired;
   (b) obtain Authorization before completing any Transaction;
   (c) where prompted, obtain the Cardholder's signature on the charge slip and compare it with the signature on the Card;
   (d) deliver a true and complete copy of the charge slip to the Cardholder (physically or digitally);
   (e) not ask the Cardholder to reveal the PIN or password;
   (f) ensure each charge slip results from a bona fide purchase of goods/services for the total stated amount, and is not the result of any fraudulent transaction.

8.5 Non-completion of the requirements in Clause 8.4 may render the charge invalid, and the Partner shall refund the entire amount on demand, irrespective of whether goods were supplied or services delivered.

8.6 The Partner shall not process any Transaction for obtaining or providing a cash advance, nor split a single invoice into multiple transactions or swipe a single Customer's card multiple times to earn revenue or circumvent limits.

8.7 **International Cards.** Where International Cards are accepted, the Partner shall collect and retain, and make available to the Company on demand: (a) a copy of the Customer's passport and visa; (b) the invoice; and (c) the front-side photo/copy of the Card used.

10.6 The Partner shall maintain accurate bank account details; the Company is not liable for delays or wrong-account credits caused by incorrect details provided by the Partner.

10.7 Settlement reports shall be made available on the dashboard/API; the Partner shall reconcile daily and raise disputes within three (3) Business Days, failing which the records shall be deemed accepted. The Company may adjust past Settlements for errors, duplicate credits, or post-facto Chargebacks.

10.8 TDS/TCS, if applicable, shall be deducted as per Applicable Law and certificates provided as required.
